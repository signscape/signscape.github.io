Bridging Silence with Vision

Welcome to an initiative designed to transform the way people who have recently experienced hearing loss communicate.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Communication challenges often isolate those who have recently become deaf, hindering their integration into deaf communities. This project seeks to address these challenges through innovative technology.

Project Objectives
  1. Develop a Robust Recognition System

  2. Enhance Communication for the Recently Deaf

  3. Improve Inclusivity in Educational Settings

  4. Real-time Detection with User-Friendly Interface
  5. Ensure Privacy and Ethical Compliance
  6. Pave the Way for Future Enhancements
